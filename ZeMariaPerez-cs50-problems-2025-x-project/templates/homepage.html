Welcome to the Wine Manager!

Wine Manager is your personal tool for building and managing your wine collection. Easily create a detailed catalog of wines, track your real-life inventory, and record your sales with just a few clicks. Whether you're managing a restaurant's wine cellar or your private collection, Wine Manager helps you stay organized and in control.

Register

Add new wines to your catalog by entering key details such as wine name, brand, region, year, and more. Create a comprehensive list of wines that you can easily browse and manage.

Catalog

Browse and filter through your registered wines. Select a wine to view its details, add it to your inventory, or remove it from your catalog.

Inventory

Keep track of your wine stock. Easily add bottles from your catalog to your inventory and register sales as they happen to maintain an accurate stock count.

Sales

Review a detailed sales history. Track when and which wines were sold, ensuring a complete and organized sales record.

Analytics

Gain insights into your wine sales and inventory trends with visual analytics. Track sales performance and inventory fluctuations to make data-driven decisions.
